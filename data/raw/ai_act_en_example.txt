CHAPTER 1
GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Regulation lays down harmonised rules on artificial intelligence in the European Union.
It establishes a list of prohibited AI practices and sets obligations for providers and deployers
of high-risk AI systems and general-purpose AI models.

CHAPTER 2
DEFINITIONS

“AI system” means a machine-based system designed to operate with varying levels of autonomy
that can, for explicit or implicit objectives, generate outputs such as predictions, content, recommendations.
